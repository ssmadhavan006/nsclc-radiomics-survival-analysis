# References and Citations

This document compiles the scientific literature, data repositories, and standards referenced in this project.

## 1. Primary Data Citation
Every publication using this dataset must cite the following primary Cancer Imaging Archive (TCIA) resource:

*   **Data Citation**:
    Aerts, H. J. W. L., Wee, L., Rios Velazquez, E., Leijenaar, R. T. H., Parmar, C., Grossmann, P., Carvalho, S., Bussink, J., Monshouwer, R., Haibe-Kains, B., Rietveld, D., Hoebers, F., Rietbergen, M. M., Leemans, C. R., Dekker, A., Quackenbush, J., Gillies, R. J., Lambin, P. (2014). Data From NSCLC-Radiomics (version 4) [Data set]. The Cancer Imaging Archive. https://doi.org/10.7937/K9/TCIA.2015.PF0M9REI

## 2. Core Methodology References
*   **Aerts et al. 2014 (Nature Communications)**:
    Aerts, H. J., Velazquez, E. R., Leijenaar, R. T., Parmar, C., Grossmann, P., Carvalho, S., ... & Lambin, P. (2014). Decoding tumour phenotype by noninvasive imaging using a quantitative radiomics approach. *Nature Communications*, 5(1), 1-9. https://doi.org/10.1038/ncomms5006
*   **Image Biomarker Standardization Initiative (IBSI)**:
    Zwanenburg, A., Vallières, M., Abdalah, M. A., Aerts, H. J., Andrearczyk, V., Apte, A., ... & Löck, S. (2020). The image biomarker standardization initiative: standardized quantitative image informatics for personalized medicine. *Radiology*, 295(2), 328-338. https://doi.org/10.1148/radiol.2020191145
*   **PyRadiomics Framework**:
    van Griethuysen, J. J., Fedorov, A., Parmar, C., Hosny, A., Aucoin, N., Narayan, V., ... & Aerts, H. J. (2017). Computational platform for expansion of imaging biomarkers as needed for cancer radiomics. *Cancer Research*, 77(21), e104-e107. https://doi.org/10.1158/0008-5472.CAN-17-0339

## 3. Statistical and Survival Modeling Literature
*   **Cox Proportional Hazards Model**:
    Cox, D. R. (1972). Regression models and life-tables. *Journal of the Royal Statistical Society: Series B (Methodological)*, 34(2), 187-202.
*   **LASSO Regularization for Survival Analysis**:
    Tibshirani, R. (1997). The lasso method for variable selection in the Cox model. *Statistics in Medicine*, 16(4), 385-395.
*   **Benjamini-Hochberg FDR Control**:
    Benjamini, Y., & Hochberg, Y. (1995). Controlling the false discovery rate: a practical and powerful approach to multiple testing. *Journal of the Royal Statistical Society: Series B (Methodological)*, 57(1), 289-300.
